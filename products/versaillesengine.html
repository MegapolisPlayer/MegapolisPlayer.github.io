Introducing Versailles

The 2D framework with everything a developer needs.

Simple and Fast

Versailles has many functions to get you up and running in a few minutes at most, while keeping performance suitable for any medium-to-high performant application.

Numerous easy-to-use abstractions

From complex multithreading through events to batch rendering, Versailles has abstractions for you at every step of the way.

Every error, logged

Versailles features 2 complementary error handling systems (one extra planned) to catch most (if not all) or the errors that might occur.
